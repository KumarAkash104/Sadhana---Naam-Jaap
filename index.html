Privacy Policy for Sadhana - Naam Jaap

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Sadhana - Naam Jaap respects your privacy. The app works primarily offline and does not collect personal information.

Information Storage

Chant history, settings, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Permissions

  • Microphone - For optional custom voice recordings.
  • Vibration - For chanting feedback.
  • Audio Controls - For devotional sounds and playback.
  • Bluetooth - For audio output through connected devices.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data.
